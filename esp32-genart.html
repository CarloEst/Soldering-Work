ESP32 Generative Art

Creative Embedded Systems - Spring 2026

Short Description

This is a generative art project of a Haitian poem using an ESP32 with a TFT display

Reflection

When it came to my first generative art project, I wanted to create something that was unique to me and my culture. I decided to create a generative art project of a Haitian song using an ESP32 with a TFT display.
I took the first paragraph of the song "Ayiti se" by Mikaben and translated it to English. The lyrics speaks about the landscapes, beaches, rivers and monuments that make Haiti the unique country that it is.
The art in the background is a reflection of the lyrics, as it shows the landscapes, the sea, the mountains and the tropical sunsets that Haiti is known for. We also have shooting stars to give this sense of magic and wonder that the lyrics evoke.
